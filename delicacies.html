Discover the Flavors of Luuk, Sulu

Piyanggang Manok

Piyanggang Manok is a delicious chicken dish marinated in a rich blend of spices and cooked with burnt coconut.

Beef Kulma

Beef Kulma is a flavorful beef curry with a creamy coconut sauce, perfect for spice lovers.

Tiyula Itum

Tiyula Itum is a unique black soup made with beef or goat, known for its distinctive burnt coconut flavor.
